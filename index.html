Welcome to Ombra

In the heart of Santurce, San Juan, Ombra Italian Eatery brings you an unparalleled dining experience where tradition meets innovation. Our name, meaning "shadow" in Italian, reflects our philosophy of creating depth through subtle contrasts—light and dark, classic and modern, simplicity and sophistication.

Recognized as one of Puerto Rico's top 10 restaurants, we pride ourselves on authentic Italian cuisine crafted with the finest local and imported ingredients, paired with an extensive wine selection from our cellar of over 250 international labels.

Chef Alfredo Esposito

With roots in Abruzzo, Italy, and a prestigious Michelin star to his name, Chef Alfredo Esposito brings a 130-year family culinary legacy to every dish. His journey from the hills of Italy to the vibrant streets of San Juan has been marked by innovation, passion, and an unwavering commitment to authenticity.

At Ombra, Chef Esposito masterfully blends traditional Italian recipes with Mediterranean influences, creating a menu that tells the story of generations while celebrating the fresh, local ingredients of Puerto Rico.

"Cooking is about honoring the past while creating something new for the future."

— Chef Alfredo Esposito
